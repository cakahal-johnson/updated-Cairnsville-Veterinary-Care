Why It's So Important to Desex Your Pet 17 November 2022
Why is it so important to desex your pet?

Responsible pet ownership means making choices that help you and your companion animal (pet) have a safe and happy life. Desexing is a very important part of this and it is crucial to understand the role that desexing plays for the health, safety and protection of your companion animal.
Desexing animals not only helps to prevent unwanted pregnancies – meaning fewer unwanted animals - but also research shows that desexed animals can actually live longer and healthier lives. Desexing can reduce the risk of certain diseases and behavioural issues, benefiting your animal’s health and welfare. Read more >>>

4 ways to keep your dog cool this summer

Now that summer is here and the temperatures are climbing in many parts of the country, it’s important to protect your dog from the heat, as exposure to high temperatures can lead to heat exhaustion and life threatening heatstroke.
Like in humans, heatstroke occurs when an animal’s body temperature becomes elevated above the normal range due to exposure to excessively high temperatures, to the point where their body’s temperature-regulating mechanism fails and they can’t maintain a healthy temperature.
